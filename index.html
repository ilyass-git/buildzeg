Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions

What types of projects do you undertake?

+

We undertake all types of architectural and construction projects: luxury villas, commercial buildings, offices, complete renovations, and fit-out projects. Our expertise covers design, construction, and renovation.

What is the average duration of a project?

+

The duration varies according to the complexity of the project. A renovation project can take 2-4 months, while a complete construction may require 6-12 months. We always provide a detailed schedule during the planning phase.

How do you get a personalized quote?

+

Contact us via our online form or by phone. We will organize a free on-site visit to assess your needs and provide you with a detailed and personalized quote within 48 hours.

Do you work throughout Morocco?

+

Yes, we operate throughout Morocco. Our teams are mobile and we have local partners in major cities. Travel expenses are included in our quotes for projects outside Casablanca.
